MOON KNIGHT

Moon Knight follows Steven Grant, a mild-mannered gift-shop employee,
who becomes plagued with blackouts and memories of another life. Steven
discovers he has dissociative identity disorder and shares a body with
mercenary Marc Spector. As Steven/Marc's enemies converge upon them,
they must navigate their complex identities while thrust into a deadly
mystery among the powerful gods of Egypt.
